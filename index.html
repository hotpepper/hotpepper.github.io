Speed Camera Program Crash Analysis

This map was created to provide support for NYCDOT to lobby for a change in legislation for additional speed cameras. Following presentaiton to the State Legislator New York City was granted permission to drammitically expand the speed camera program

This required mapping the streets where cameras were legally permissible and calculating if a crash would be covered in the permissible areas and times of the existing program. Cameras were only permitted on streets within a quarter mile of a school and ajacent to school entrances.
